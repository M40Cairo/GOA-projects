National Teams

Men's Teams

  • Brazil
    • Achievements: Olympic gold medals (1992, 2004, 2016), multiple World Championships, World League titles
    • Notable Players: Giba, Bruno Rezende, Ricardo Lucarelli
  • United States
    • Achievements: Olympic gold medals (1984, 1988, 2008), several World Cup and World League titles
    • Notable Players: Karch Kiraly, Matt Anderson, Micah Christenson
  • Russia
    • Achievements: Olympic gold medals (1964, 1968, 1980, 2012 as part of the Soviet Union and Russia), numerous World Championships and European Championships
    • Notable Players: Sergey Tetyukhin, Maxim Mikhaylov, Dmitry Muserskiy

Major Competitions

  • Olympic Games
    • Frequency: Every four years
    • Description: Features the top national teams from around the world, divided into men’s and women’s tournaments
  • FIVB World Championships
    • Frequency: Every four years
    • Description: National teams compete for the world title in both men's and women's categories
  • FIVB Volleyball Nations League (VNL)
    • Frequency: Annually
    • Description: Replaced the World League (men) and World Grand Prix (women). Features top national teams in a league format
  • CEV Champions League
    • Frequency: Annually
    • Description: Prestigious European club competition featuring the best teams from domestic leagues across Europe
  • FIVB Club World Championship
    • Frequency: Annually
    • Description: Top club teams from various continents compete to be crowned world champions
